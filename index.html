Our Story

Few words about us

In 2015, the design of the Biswa Bangla Gate was published for the first time within the public circle. It was 6th March 2017 when the construction work of the 'Kolkata Gate' started. The expected budget for this restaurant was around 25 crores, which was sanctioned under the beautification project of New Town.

Biswa Bangla Gate (also known as Kolkata Gate) is an arch-monument in the city of New Town, Kolkata, West Bengal,India.
